The Massively Multiplayer Piano

A Music Hack Day: London 2013 hack by Alex Pounds (@acreature).

  • Your TEAM has to play a CHORD.
  • You can only play one NOTE at a TIME. Press and HOLD.
  • Your notes will show up in YELLOW. Your team-mates' presses will show up in BLUE.
  • Easy levels have hints in RED.
  • Your team must play the CHORD before the timer runs OUT.
